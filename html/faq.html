Q. What is hand poked tattoo?


A. Hand poked tattoo is a method of tattooing that uses a single needle that is repeatedly dipped into ink and then poked into the skin. Lines and dots are built up to create a tattoo.


Q. How do I make a booking with you?


A. You can book online here or send me a message on Instagram @pokeyinstokey . Generally there is waiting list of a week.

Q. Can I get a tattoo that you have already done?


A. Most of my designs are unique and not for repeated use. However I can design something simular to my previous work.

Q. How much does a tattoo cost from you?


A. Prices vary on design location, size, detail and design time. It is best to get in contact for an estimate. Custom designs will incur a small drawing fee.

Q. Is there anything you don't tattoo?


A. I simply won't tattoo anything that is offensive.

Q. Are there any spots on the body that you won't tattoo?


A. I avoid tattooing ribs and inner biceps as they have a higher risk of blow out. I will tattoo simple designs on neck, head and hands.

Q. If I need to cancel my appointment can I get my deposit back.


A. Due to the time that it takes to design and schedule tattoos deposits are non refundable. If you contact with 48 hours notice your appointment may be able to be rescheduled.
